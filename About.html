About

Dinora Young, a realtor, originally hailing from El Salvador, embarked on her journey to the United States in 2001, settling in Salt Lake County with her family of five, including three boys and two girls. Driven by ambitious dreams, she began by immersing herself in English classes, eventually earning a certificate in accounting and an associate degree in Business from a community college. With a solid educational foundation, Dinora embarked on a career path that led her to excel as a logistics supervisor for many years. Her journey then took her to Beyond's headquarters, where she served as the transportation Outbound Manager in the Supply Chain department. Transitioning to real estate in 2019, Dinora found her true calling in helping individuals achieve their dreams of homeownership. Beyond her professional pursuits, Dinora finds joy in the great outdoors, indulging in activities like camping and hiking. She also has a passion for dancing Zumba and admires flowers, particularly roses. A deeply passionate individual, Dinora derives fulfillment from assisting others in realizing their aspirations of owning a home.
